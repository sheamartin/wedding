Welcome to Atlanta

By Air

The closest and most convenient airport is Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL).

From ATL, Newnan is approximately a 40–45 minute drive south depending on traffic.

  • Rental cars are widely available at ATL
  • Rideshare is available but distance-based pricing applies

By Land

If you’re driving, we recommend staying in Newnan or nearby cities in Coweta County rather than Atlanta.

Atlanta traffic can be unpredictable—staying closer makes wedding day smoother and more relaxed.
